Known Bugs

  1. Transferring ownership will create a reference to every file and folder that you transfer in your root drive.
    You can eliminate the additional references by simply dragging the copied at the "My Drive" level into the intended folder. This will eliminate the My Drive references to the file.
    Reference
  2. Transferring ownership of anything except Google Drive files (e.g. PDFs, images, plain text files, etc) will not work.
    Reference:
    1. Go to
    2. Enter the fileID for a file in your drive that is a PDF, image, etc.
    3. In the "Request Body", enter this with the email you'd like to transfer to: 
                            "role": "owner",
                      "type": "user",
                      "value": "[[email to transfer to]]"
    4. You will get a 400 response

How do I undo the transfer of ownership?

The transfer of ownership cannot be undone. If you want to create a personal copy of the folder that is not shared with anyone else, you can use the Copy Folder tool to create an individual copy of the folder.
Top

The transfer has been paused longer than 2 minutes and it isn't complete. What do I do?

When the app stops, you can use the "Resume" button to restart the transfer.
